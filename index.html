Open Garden is a social medium running on Earth time and Solar power, designed to compost the failures of platform capitalism and plant new intentions as our own social technologists.

The live cam streams a durational performance by the garden in collaboration with the artist, the score evolving each week based on an assemblage of instructions collectively generated by site visitors. Alt text can be contributed as both a practice of crowdsourced accessibility and of bearing witness to the present moment.

The performance will run until end of May.

---

Open Garden is created by Alice Yuan Zhang, a Culturehub resident artist. She is an artist, educator, and immigrant based on tongva, chumash, and kizh land, exploring ecology, networks, and interspecies collaboration. web development by tong wu and bhavik singh sound by alexander babbitt thanks to folks at culturehub, surface tension, and virtual care lab for your patient support and feedback.
